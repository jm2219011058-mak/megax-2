Execution Roadmap

From Silicon to Scale

A disciplined six-year plan moving from MPW validation to mass production across all product lines — with break-even projected by 2028.

Q1–Q2 2026

Production Ignition

Wi-Fi 6 SoC enters mass production on GlobalFoundries 22nm. HaLow G-Link and Freya Transceiver complete design optimization. Engineering teams for Wi-Fi 7 programs onboarded.

Q3–Q4 2026

First Revenue Milestones

HaLow G-Link and Freya Transceiver begin mass production on TSMC nodes. First commercial revenue from HaLow deployments. FCC/CE/SRRC certifications filed.

2027

Wi-Fi 7 Silicon Arrives

Wi-Fi 7 4×4 AP Baseband and 2×2 AP SoC reach mass production. Robotic Joint Neural Network module sales begin. Revenue ramp across all active product lines.

2028

Break-Even & Scale

Company reaches profitability. Full six-product portfolio generating revenue at scale. Base-case revenue projection: ~$85M.

2029–2031

Global Expansion

Revenue scales toward $1.5B target by 2031, positioning Mega X as a top-tier wireless chipset company globally.
