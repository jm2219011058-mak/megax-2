About Mega X

The Team Behind
the Silicon

We are semiconductor veterans, RF engineers, and system architects united by a single mission: making the world's wireless infrastructure smarter, faster, and more secure.

Our Mission

Secure Connectivity at Every Scale

High-end RF chip development requires deep, long-term expertise across analog design, RF engineering, and system architecture. Our team brings decades of combined experience from leading global semiconductor firms.

We design for the full wireless spectrum — from ultra-low-power sub-1 GHz HaLow sensors to high-throughput Wi-Fi 7 enterprise access points — with built-in hardware security and edge intelligence that traditional chipmakers can't match.
